## Ethical Standards
JASQAI follows COPE (Committee on Publication Ethics) guidelines.

## Peer Review Policy
- Double-blind peer review  
- Minimum 2 reviewers  
- Editor-in-Chief has final decision authority  

## Open Access Policy
All articles are freely available without any fees.

## Copyright & Licensing
Authors retain copyright.  
Published articles will use **Creative Commons (CC BY 4.0)** license.

## Conflict of Interest
Authors must disclose financial or professional conflicts.
