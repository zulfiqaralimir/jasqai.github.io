# Journal Policies  
**JASQAI – Journal of Agentic Synergy & Quantum AI**

The following policies ensure the integrity, transparency, and academic quality of all publications.

---

# 1. Publication Ethics

JASQAI adheres to international guidelines such as **COPE (Committee on Publication Ethics)**.

Authors must ensure:

- Research is original and unpublished  
- No ethical misconduct or data tampering  
- Proper attribution and citations  
- All authors have contributed meaningfully  
- Conflicts of interest are declared  

Editors and reviewers must act with neutrality and confidentiality at all times.

---

# 2. Plagiarism Policy

Plagiarism is strictly prohibited.

JASQAI uses automated plagiarism screening tools and manual checks.  
Submissions with:

- Copy–paste text  
- Self-plagiarism  
- Uncited paraphrasing  

may be rejected without review.

Threshold: **Less than 10% similarity** recommended.

---

# 3. Peer Review Policy

All submissions follow a **double-blind peer review process**, meaning:

- Authors do not know the reviewers  
- Reviewers do not know the authors  

Steps:

1. Editorial screening  
2. Assignment to 2–3 expert reviewers  
3. Review reports returned in 2–4 weeks  
4. Authors submit revisions  
5. Final decision by Editor-in-Chief  

Possible outcomes: Accept, Minor Revision, Major Revision, Reject.

---

# 4. Data Availability Policy

Authors must ensure:

- Data used in the study is available upon request  
- Code repositories are linked (GitHub preferred)  
- Sensitive data may be anonymized  
- Reproducibility is encouraged  

For AI and FinTech research, transparency is essential.

---

# 5. Retraction & Correction Policy

Papers may be **corrected** or **retracted** if:

- Major errors are discovered  
- Ethical issues arise  
- Plagiarism is identified  
- Data falsification is proven  

Corrections include:

- Erratum (publisher mistake)  
- Corrigendum (author mistake)  

Retractions include a signed editorial notice.

---

# 6. Copyright & Licensing

Authors retain copyright.  
JASQAI recommends **Creative Commons CC BY 4.0** for open access.

This allows:

- Sharing  
- Reuse  
- Adaptation  

as long as proper citation is provided.

---

# 7. Conflict of Interest Policy

Authors must declare:

- Funding sources  
- Institutional affiliations  
- Personal or financial interests  

Editors must recuse themselves from handling submissions with conflicts.

---

# 8. Withdrawal Policy

Authors may withdraw a paper **before peer review**.  
Once review starts, a formal withdrawal request is required.

Accepted papers cannot be withdrawn.

---

# 9. Archiving Policy

JASQAI uses:

- GitHub version control  
- Zenodo for long-term archiving (coming soon)  
- DOIs for permanent citation  

All published articles remain accessible indefinitely.

---

# 10. Open Access Policy

JASQAI is fully **open access**, meaning:

- No paywalls  
- No subscription fees  
- Authors retain rights  
- Readers access all content free of charge

Funded by community, donations, and voluntary contributions.

---

For questions about policies, contact: **editor@jasqai.org**
